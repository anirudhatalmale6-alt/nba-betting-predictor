### How to Read This

- **MONEYLINE**: Picking the underdog to win outright. Odds show the payout (e.g., +200 = $100 bet wins $200)
- **SPREAD (ATS)**: Picking the underdog to cover the point spread (e.g., +5.5 means they can lose by up to 5)
- **TOTAL**: Picking whether the combined score goes OVER or UNDER the line
- **Edge**: How much the model's probability exceeds the market's (positive = value)
- **Confidence**: LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH based on edge size
